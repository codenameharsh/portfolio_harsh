AURA: Ethereal Dreams

Case Study | Brand Identity & Lookbook Design

Role: Brand Designer, Illustrator

Duration: 4 Weeks

Introduction

Client: AURA is a luxury fashion brand rooted in celestial elegance, soft femininity, and modern mysticism. They approached me to design a cohesive brand style guide and an editorial-style lookbook for their newest limited-edition collection: Ethereal Dreams. The goal was to build a visual identity that bridges luxury and lightness, while maintaining commercial usability.

Client Insights & Creative Direction

Our initial discovery conversations revealed three critical insights that shaped the entire project:

  • Luxury That Feels Light

    AURA’s creative team emphasized the importance of graceful elegance — a brand that speaks to the modern woman with softness and strength. The design needed to feel luxurious, yet grounded in calm and clarity.

  • Editorial Meets Functional

    The lookbook needed to evoke an editorial feel (think Kinfolk, Vogue) but also function as a sales tool for buyers, stylists, and boutique retailers. It had to inspire and inform.

  • Celestial Symbolism

    Moons, stars, constellations, and ethereal linework were essential visual motifs — subtle, sophisticated, and woven naturally into the layout and visual language.

Research & Discovery

  • Understanding the Brand

    I began with a deep dive into AURA’s vision, mission, and emotional anchors. I wanted the brand to not just be seen—but felt.Key emotional descriptors that shaped my approach: mystical, graceful, otherworldly, empowering.

  • Audience Persona

    Demographic: Women aged 25–45

    Psychographic: Aesthetically inclined, values intentional design, emotionally driven shoppers who see fashion as a form of storytelling and identity.

  • Competitive Inspiration

    I analyzed brands like Elie Saab, Simone Rocha, Rodarte, and Zuhair Murad, noting how they use visual fantasy and poetic themes to create immersive narratives.

  • Thematic Exploration

    Moodboards were created with the following in mind:

    • Celestial bodies, constellations, moonlight
    • Flowing textures (velvet, silk), soft gradients, metallic shimmer
    • Surrealist editorials, cosmic art, star maps

Design Process

  1. Discovery & Moodboarding

    Using the client’s aesthetic direction, I created an interactive moodboard reflecting:

    • Celestial imagery
    • Soft, flowing fabrics
    • High-contrast editorial layouts
    • Elegant serif typefaces with whimsical details

    After client feedback, we refined the direction to emphasize cool color palettes (blues, purples, silvers) and subtle shimmer textures, aligning with the collection's tones.

  2. Visual Identity & Style Guide

    The style guide served as a foundation for future branding. Key components included:

    • Typography: I chose Major Mono Display for headlines—its graceful curves and minimal, modern style captured the elegance AURA sought. Body copy used was Arapey, for timeless its readability.
    • Color Palette: Golden Honey (FFCC66), Sunlit Cream (FFCC99), Cerulean Mist (36586C), Midnight Sky (284F70), Navy Twilight (182D52), Velvet Noir (15193C)
    • Icons & Motifs: Custom-drawn minimalist celestial symbols — moons, stars, constellation lines — used in layout dividers, footer details, and behind-text overlays. These were sketched in Procreate, vectorized in Illustrator, and styled to match the tone of the typography.
  3. Lookbook Design: Ethereal Dreams Collection

    The lookbook was designed as both a tactile and digital piece. I approached it as an editorial spread that told the story of the collection through atmosphere and pacing.

      Layout Principles:
    • Full-bleed images with poetic overlays and product annotations
    • Clean grid-based design for product details
    • White space as breathing room to highlight the elegance of each piece
    • Star icons used as stylish bullet points for product features (e.g., fit, fabric, craftsmanship)
    • Foil stamping and shimmer paper mockups for potential print embellishments

    Each chapter of the lookbook began with a celestial quote — a client request that helped tie the theme into the reader's emotional journey.

  4. Collaboration & Refinement

    AURA’s creative team and I held weekly check-ins to review:

    • Layout pacing
    • Visual hierarchy
    • Print-ready specifications and finishings

    Their input led to refined print adjustments: soft matte paper stock with foil accents on the cover and spine, resulting in a piece that felt both tactile and luxurious.

Outcome

The final result was a timeless visual guide and a compelling lookbook that encapsulated the dreamlike vision of the AURA brand. We revealed the collection at a private artisan fashion preview, hosted at an independent boutique in Chennai — an intimate space that mirrored the ethereal elegance and handcrafted ethos of AURA.

Since launch, the brand has commissioned additional digital versions for influencer outreach and seasonal press kits.

Personal Reflections

This project challenged and deepened my appreciation for editorial storytelling and brand collaboration. Blending celestial minimalism with tactile luxury allowed me to explore a space where design feels almost meditative — a gentle balance of clarity and wonder.
