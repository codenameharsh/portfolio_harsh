About Me

Design. Code. Create.

Hey, I’m Harshitha—a visual storyteller, creative thinker, and all-around design enthusiast. I blend graphic design, branding, and UI/UX to create digital experiences that are not only beautiful but meaningful. My journey started at the intersection of fashion and art, where I realized how much I love bringing ideas to life—visually, emotionally, and sometimes, even with a bit of code.


Since then, I’ve had the chance to work with some amazing brands and startups— designing logos, building brand identities, illustrating infographics, putting together lookbooks, and crafting interactive user interfaces. Along the way, I got curious about what happens behind the screen too. That curiosity led me into front-end development and full-stack JavaScript , through platforms like Meta, IBM (Coursera), FreeCodeCamp, The Odin Project, and DailyUI.

A little timeline of my journey:

  • 2017 - Jumped into the fashion world, diving into:
    • Fashion Management
    • Digital Design
    • Business Marketing
  • 2022 - Shifted gears into UI/UX and Front-End Dev, learning how to:
    • Design thoughtful, user-centered interfaces
    • Build interactive, engaging digital experiences
  • Now – I'm a Graphic & UI Designer focused on:
    • Branding & Identity
    • Responsive Web Design
    • Vector Graphics & Infographics

Outside of design, I’m probably nose-deep in a book, lost in a painting, scribbling poetry, hunting down the spiciest food on the menu, or re-reading Harry Potter for the hundredth time. ("After all this time?" "Always.")


At the heart of it all, I’m someone who loves creating things that feel intentional—designs that tell a story, solve a problem, or make someone’s day just a little bit better.
